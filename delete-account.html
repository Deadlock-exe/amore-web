Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to always.miamore@gmail.com with the subject line: Request Account Deletion.
  2. In the email, include the following information:
    • Your registered email address.
    • Any additional information to verify your identity (if applicable).
  3. We will review your request and delete your account and data within 7 business days.

Note: Certain data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes, as specified in our Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at always.miamore@gmail.com.
